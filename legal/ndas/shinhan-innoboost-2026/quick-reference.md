# NDA Quick Reference — Shinhan Innoboost 2026

> For every squad member. Read this before touching any Shinhan-related materials.

---

## What's Covered Under NDA

Everything Shinhan shares with us and everything we share with Shinhan:

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Shinhan-supplied data** | Synthetic datasets, masked data, schema specifications |
| **Shinhan credentials** | Pilot-environment access, SDK keys, VPN configs |
| **Shinhan architecture** | Internal system designs disclosed during interviews |
| **Shinhan roadmap** | Internal priorities, timelines, org structure |
| **CyberSkill AI Doctrine** | Our internal AI governance framework |
| **CyberSkill code patterns** | Reused patterns from Engagement A & B (subject to sponsor consent) |
| **CyberSkill commercial strategy** | Pricing, commercial-track plans, competitive positioning |

## What's NOT Covered

- Information already publicly available (e.g., Shinhan's public website, annual reports)
- Information you independently developed without reference to disclosed material
- Information lawfully received from a third party without breach

---

## The Five Rules

### 1. 🔒 Don't share Shinhan info outside the squad
Not with other CyberSkill team members, not with friends, not with family, not on social media.

### 2. 🚫 Don't commit secrets to source control
Shinhan-supplied data, credentials, and system prompts do NOT go in git — not even in private repos. Use the designated encrypted secrets store.

### 3. 🔍 Don't try to re-identify masked data
Even if you think you can figure out who/what the masked data represents. This is explicitly prohibited.

### 4. 📵 Don't discuss on public forums
No Twitter, LinkedIn, conference talks, or blog posts about the Shinhan engagement without founder approval.

### 5. 🗣️ Use the NDA redirect phrase
If a casual conversation drifts into Confidential Information:

> *"I'd love to discuss that under our NDA — let me get that in front of you."*

| Scenario | What to Do |
|---|---|
| A Shinhan reviewer asks for a different NDA template | Redirect to legal lead. Do not negotiate on the spot. |
| You receive Shinhan data via email | Forward to the designated secure channel. Do not save locally. |

| You see a Slack message mentioning Shinhan internal details | Do not screenshot or forward outside the squad channel. |
| You want to blog about a technical pattern from the project | Get founder approval first. Even generic patterns might be identifiable. |
| A counterparty asks you to sign a different NDA | Redirect to legal lead. Never sign anything yourself. |
