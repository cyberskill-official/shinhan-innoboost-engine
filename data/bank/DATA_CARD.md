## Sensitivity Mix (RBAC Demo)

Deliberate sensitivity distribution per row:
- **Public**: 50% of P&L/position rows
- **Internal**: 35%
- **Restricted**: 15%

This enables demonstration of RBAC filtering where different roles see different subsets.

## Known Limitations

- P&L figures are randomly generated, not derived from lending/deposit balances
- FX MTM P&L doesn't correlate with actual rate movements
- Incident categories are uniformly distributed, not reflecting real patterns
