# Data Card — Securities (SS1)

## Provenance

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Source** | Synthetic (generated by CyberSkill data pipeline) |
| **Business Unit** | Securities (Shinhan Securities Vietnam) |
| **Generation Method** | Seeded pseudorandom with Faker-VN |
| **Seed** | 42 (default) |
| **Reproducible** | Yes — same seed produces identical output |
| **Contains Real PII** | No — all identifiers use `SYN-` prefix |
| **Total Rows** | ~5.3M+ |
| **Tables** | 8 |

## Tables

| Table | Rows | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `customers` | ~20,000 | Securities customers (individual 85%, institutional 10%, foreign 5%) |
| `accounts` | ~25,000 | Cash and margin trading accounts |
| `holdings` | ~150,000 | Current stock holdings with unrealised P&L |
| `trades` | ~5,000,000 | Trade records (1-min aggregated from intraday tick) |
| `portfolios` | 5,000 | Named portfolios with strategy labels |
| `watchlists` | 30,000 | Customer watchlists with optional price alerts |
| `research_notes` | 500 | Analyst research notes (buy/hold/sell) |
| `market_data` | ~62,500 | Daily OHLCV for HOSE/HNX symbols (5 years) |

## Stock Universe

- **HOSE**: 40 symbols (VNM, VCB, VHM, VIC, HPG, FPT, etc.)
- **HNX**: 10 symbols (SHS, PVS, CEO, etc.)
- Trading hours: realistic VN market sessions
- Order types: LO, MP, ATO, ATC

## Known Limitations

- Market data uses random walk, not correlated with real historical prices
- Trade volumes are uniformly distributed, not reflecting market microstructure
- Holdings don't perfectly reconcile with trade history
- Research notes are placeholder summaries
