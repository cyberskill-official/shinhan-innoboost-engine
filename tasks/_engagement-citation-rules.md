## Ground Rules

1. **Never name either customer by company name.** Not in conversation, not in slides, not in Slack, not in screenshots.
2. **Use only the authorised phrasings below.** Do not paraphrase, round differently, or add colour commentary.
3. **If a Shinhan reviewer asks a question that goes beyond these phrasings**, say: *"I'd be happy to explore that on a reference call with the sponsor — let me arrange that."*
4. **If you are unsure whether a fact is authorised**, do not share it. Ask sales lead first.

These are the most common accidental slips:

| ❌ Do NOT say | ✅ Say instead |
|---|---|
| "Our client {Company Name}…" | "One of our reference engagements…" |

| "Their PDPL audit was clean" | "PDPL audit passed with zero findings on the AI-touched workflow" |

## Fallback — If a Sponsor Declines

If either sponsor declines consent (or consent is revoked after signing):

1. Do NOT cite any metric from that engagement
2. Notify sales lead and founder immediately
3. Affected materials (pitch decks, FAQ doc, reference one-pagers) are redlined within 48 hours
4. Never bluff — do not cite a sponsor without consent, even under pressure during the interview
